## 1Take v1.3.2 Is Here!

**1Take v1.3.2** is now available on the App Store. This update focuses on one of the most requested features: **flexible export options**.

---

## What's New

### MP3/AAC Export (Pro)

Export your recordings in compressed formats for easy sharing.

- **MP3** - Universal compatibility. Perfect for sharing via social media and messaging apps
- **AAC** - Better quality than MP3 at the same bitrate. Great for Apple ecosystem
- **Bitrate selection** - Choose the right balance between file size and audio quality

No more "file too large" errors when sharing your recordings.

### Batch Export (Pro)

Export multiple recordings at once.

- Enter edit mode in the recording list and select the recordings you want
- Choose your format and export them all in one go
- Send rehearsal recordings to your bandmates in seconds

No more exporting files one by one.

### High-Quality Export (Pro)

Export in 24-bit WAV/BWF for maximum audio fidelity.

- **24-bit WAV** - High-resolution audio beyond CD quality
- **24-bit BWF** - Embedded timecode for seamless DAW integration

When you're taking recordings to mixing and mastering, quality matters.

---

## Improved Pro Feature Discovery

We've made it easier for free users to discover Pro features.

- **Tap any locked feature** → Go directly to the Pro upgrade screen
- One-tap access from file format settings, preset selection, and all Pro features
- Animated purchase button for clear upgrade path

No more guessing how to unlock features.

---

## Stability Improvements

- **Audio session interruption handling** - Better recovery from phone calls and Siri activation
- **Data race fixes** - Full compliance with Swift 6 strict concurrency checking
- **AudioPlayer** - Fixed recursive call that caused stack overflow
