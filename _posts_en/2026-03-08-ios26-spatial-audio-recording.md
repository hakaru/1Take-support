## iOS 26 Transforms iPhone Audio Recording

At WWDC 2025, Apple announced sweeping improvements to audio recording in iOS 26. For creators and recording app developers alike, this is the most significant audio update in years.

### Spatial Audio Recording Goes Open

iOS 26 opens Spatial Audio recording to third-party apps via AVAssetWriter. Using the iPhone's built-in microphone array, apps can now capture 3D sound scenes in Ambisonics format — bringing immersive audio to podcasts, field recordings, and music production.

### Real-Time Audio Processing

CaptureSession now supports simultaneous MovieFileOutput and AudioDataOutput. This means apps can record while simultaneously processing and visualizing audio in real time — enabling richer feedback for creators during recording sessions.

### Audio Mix API

A new API lets apps balance foreground and background audio sources. For apps handling multiple audio streams, this provides granular mixing control that wasn't previously possible.

### AirPods as Pro-Quality Microphones

iOS 26 adds a new media tuning mode to AirPods. Instead of the call-optimized audio processing, creators can now use a mode specifically tuned for music and content creation recording.
