## Accidentally Deleted a Recording? You Can Get It Back

We've all been there — you accidentally delete an important rehearsal recording and it's gone forever.

Not anymore. Starting with **1Take v1.3.2**, deleted recordings are moved to the **Files app** instead of being permanently removed.

---

## What Changed?

### Before

Tap delete → **Gone forever**. No undo.

### With v1.3.2

Tap delete → Moved to **"Recently Deleted"** in the Files app. **Recoverable for 30 days**.

---

## How to Recover a Recording

1. Open the **Files** app on your iPhone
2. Tap **"Recently Deleted"**
3. Long-press the recording file → tap **"Recover"**

That's it. The file is restored to its original location.

---

## FAQ

**Q. What happens after 30 days?**

iOS automatically and permanently deletes the file. Make sure to recover within 30 days.

**Q. Will this use extra storage?**

Deleted recordings count toward "Recently Deleted" storage, but are automatically freed after 30 days. You can also manually delete them from the Files app if you need to free up space immediately.

**Q. Can I recover from within the 1Take app?**

Currently, recovery is only available through the Files app. Once you restore the file, it will reappear in 1Take's recording list.
