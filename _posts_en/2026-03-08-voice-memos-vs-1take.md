## 5 Reasons Musicians Should Switch to 1Take

### 1. Break Free from AGC

Voice Memos' biggest problem for musicians is **AGC (Automatic Gain Control)**.

AGC raises the gain when sound is quiet and lowers it when loud. Useful for conversations. Devastating for music.

**What happens:**
- Guitar solo gets louder → AGC pulls gain down → **dynamics crushed**
- Silence between songs → AGC pushes gain up → **noise amplified**
- Dynamic performance → AGC constantly chasing → **pumping artifacts**

1Take **doesn't use AGC**. You set the Input Trim manually, and the compressor and limiter handle dynamics musically. The same approach used by professional recording engineers.

### 2. Your Recording Sounds Finished, Not Raw

Voice Memos records the raw signal. 1Take applies a professional signal processing chain in real-time:

```
1Take signal chain:
Input → Noise Gate → EQ (5-band parametric) → Compressor → Limiter → Record
```

**Three presets for instant setup:**

- **Studio** — For acoustic instruments and vocals. LA-2A-modeled compression preserves natural dynamics
- **Studio+** — For bands and dynamic performances. 1176-modeled compression for a solid, controlled sound
- **Live** — For venues and practice rooms. Noise gate cuts MC chatter and ambient noise between songs

With Voice Memos, you need another app to process your recordings later. With 1Take, **press record and get a polished sound**.

### 3. Know Exactly How Good Your Recording Is

No more guessing "did that sound okay?"

1Take automatically analyzes your recording when you stop and shows:

- **LUFS (Loudness)** — Same standard used by Spotify and Apple Music
- **Peak Level** — Confirms whether clipping occurred
- **Crest Factor** — Evaluates dynamic range health
- **Actionable tips** — "Increase Input Trim" or "Switch to Studio preset" with specific guidance

Voice Memos offers none of this.

### 4. Never Miss the Beginning Again

"That riff was amazing... but I hadn't pressed record yet."

1Take's **Pre-roll** feature continuously buffers audio. When you hit record, the previous seconds are automatically included.

With **Auto-Record**, 1Take starts recording the moment sound begins. Intelligent calibration learns your room's noise floor to prevent false triggers.

Voice Memos has neither feature.

### 5. Pro-Quality Files Go Straight to Your DAW

Voice Memos outputs M4A (AAC lossy compression). When you import to a DAW, the information lost to compression never comes back. EQ and compression reveal ugly artifacts.

1Take outputs **lossless WAV**. With Pro, you get **24-bit BWF** with markers and metadata that carry into your DAW.

| | Voice Memos (M4A) | 1Take (WAV) | 1Take Pro (BWF) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Compression | Lossy | **Lossless** | **Lossless** |
| Bit Depth | ~16-bit | 16-bit | **24-bit** |
| DAW Editing Headroom | Low | High | **Very High** |
| Marker Data | None | None | **Yes** |
